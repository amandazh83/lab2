Main Characters

Harry James Potter, the protagonist of the series, was born on July 31, 1980. He is the only child of wizarding couple James Potter and Lily Potter. Harry has black hair and green eyes, resembling his father, and his perpetually messy hair is a trademark of the Potter family. He possesses a natural talent for flying. His pet, a snowy owl named Hedwig, was a gift from Hogwarts' Keeper of Keys and Grounds, Rubeus Hagrid, on his eleventh birthday.
Hermione Jean Granger, born on September 19, 1979, comes from a Muggle family. In the books, she is portrayed as a highly intelligent girl with a passion for academics and a know-it-all demeanor, occasionally coming across as bossy. Her extensive knowledge proves invaluable in many of their adventures. The only subject she dislikes is Divination, which she considers an "imprecise" field of study. Shortly after arriving at Hogwarts, Hermione quickly establishes herself as the brightest student in her year.
Ronald Bilius Weasley, born on March 1, 1980, is a pure-blood wizard and the sixth son of Arthur and Molly Weasley. He is also the younger brother of Bill, Charlie, Percy, Fred, George, and the elder brother of Ginny. Ron and his brothers and sister lived in The Burrow. Ron began attending Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in 1991 and was sorted into Gryffindor House. He soon became best friends with fellow student Harry Potter and later Hermione Granger. Together, they faced many challenges during their adolescence.
